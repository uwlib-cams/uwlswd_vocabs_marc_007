Sound recording: special playback characteristics

(MARC21 007/12 values for sound recordings expressed using RDF)

Playback characteristics for sound recordings, including special equipment or equalization necessary for proper playback. Not used to indicate special processes used during recording unless those processes must be applied during playback.
